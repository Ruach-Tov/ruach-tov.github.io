Polyarchitecture: Software Governance Through Boundary Specification

$20 · PDF + BND specification files

Define a service boundary once. Generate implementations in Rust, Python, Zig, and more. Require identical behavior through conformance testing and mutation analysis.

This white paper documents the methodology we used to generate a 100% generated Rust MCP bridge that passes 32 conformance tests with zero hand-edits — and prove it earns its existence through mutation analysis.

What you get is not just an article. You get the boundary DSL specification that generates the bridge, the conformance test suite, and a working example of the entire build ecosystem: generator, verifier, and traceability report.

Whole ecosystems of build tools are better fits for AI agents than typical CI/CD pipelines. If you invent enough ways to verify software, you can eventually leave your agents to work while you sleep. This paper shows you how we do that.
